NIQ Oil and Gas is one of the fastest-growing indigenous oil companies in Nigeria, Africa. Differentiated by the quality of our people and our relentless drive to deliver the most successful outcomes, we create a lasting positive impact.

MISSION

To build and sustain a world-class oil and gas company through innovative partnerships and premium value delivery.

VISION

To be a world class energy company delivering premium value to all stakeholders.

CORE VALUES

Professionalism: We provide services using industry accredited standards and practices. Commitment: We are dedicated to the success of our clients, partners, and employees Excellence: Integrity: Building a professional relationship by being truthful and honest Collaboration: As an emerging organization, we strive to create an environment where accidents are unlikely to occur and the health and safety of employees, contractors, and the public are not jeopardized.

CORPORATE SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY

NIQ Oil and Gas Corporate Social Responsibility is focused on sustainability, we believe that behaving responsibly is critical to the sustainability of our organization which is why we are committed to looking after the health safety, and development of our people. We work towards enhancing and protecting our reputation with stakeholders, to contribute positively to the host communities, and to minimize our environmental footprint. Our CSR framework is focused on providing support to members of our host communities through the Community Relations and Development Department. We have supported the improvement of infrastructure, encouraging local enterprise, facilitating know-how, and building capacity for the sustainable use of resources in our host communities, these activities have fostered harmonious relationships with our host communities. We strive for a better, stronger, and more sustainable community relationship in our areas of operation.
